I have developed a comprehensive Marketing Campaign Dashboard that transforms raw customer data into actionable business insights. I designed high-impact KPIs to track total revenue and conversion rates, while using Bar Charts to compare campaign success and product popularity. To understand customer behavior, I implemented Global Filters for demographics and created a Scatter Plot to analyze the correlation between income and spending. Additionally, I optimized the UI/UX by customizing font hierarchies and layouts for better readability and professional presentation. This project effectively identifies high-value customer segments and highlights the most profitable sales channels for the business.
