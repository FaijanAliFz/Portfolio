About Me

I am a passionate and results-driven data professional with expertise in using a wide array of tools and technologies to extract insights, optimize processes, and drive data-driven decision-making. With a strong foundation in data analysis, visualization, and management, I am committed to delivering impactful solutions tailored to business needs.

I specialize in Excel, where I design advanced models, dashboards, and inventory systems that enable dynamic decision-making. My proficiency in SQL allows me to query complex datasets, uncovering trends and insights to optimize operations, from customer behavior analysis to employee performance tracking. With Power BI, I create interactive, visually compelling dashboards that present actionable insights on everything from financial health to customer segmentation. In Tableau, I bring data to life with engaging visualizations, uncovering trends and opportunities in areas like marketing performance and sales analysis.

In the realm of Python for Data Analysis, I leverage libraries like pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn to build robust analytical tools and predictive models, tackling projects ranging from COVID-19 trend analysis to customer churn predictions. With Python for Data Visualization, I utilize Plotly, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to build interactive dashboards that simplify complex datasets, enabling effective storytelling and insights. I am proficient in BigQuery, where I analyze large-scale datasets, processing customer behavior, e-commerce trends, and public datasets efficiently with SQL. Lastly, my expertise in R includes statistical modeling, machine learning, and data visualization, providing solutions for projects like real estate price predictions and social media sentiment analysis.

I am driven by the desire to solve real-world problems and deliver actionable insights that add value to organizations. My goal is to continuously enhance my skills, contribute to business growth, and help organizations make data-informed decisions.
