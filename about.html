About Me

Hello! My name is Faijan Ali. I bring over 10 years of professional experience, including 5 years specializing in Data Analysis and 5 years as an IT Trainer where I taught data and analytics tools to students and professionals. This combination of industry practice and teaching has strengthened my ability to understand complex data problems and communicate insights effectively.

I specialize in a range of data analysis tools and technologies, including:

  • Excel for advanced data analysis, automation, and reporting
  • SQL for database querying, data extraction, and data management
  • Power BI for building interactive dashboards and business intelligence reports
  • Tableau for creating powerful data visualizations and analytics dashboards
  • Python and its visualization libraries for data analysis, automation, and insights generation
  • BigQuery for large-scale data querying and processing
  • R for statistical analysis and data modeling

Throughout my career, I have completed multiple data-driven projects focused on transforming raw data into meaningful insights, improving business processes, and supporting data-driven decision-making. My experience as both a practitioner and educator enables me to approach problems analytically while communicating findings in a clear and impactful way.

I remain committed to continuous learning and staying aligned with emerging technologies in data analytics to deliver effective solutions to complex business challenges.
