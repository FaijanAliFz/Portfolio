The Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard provides an interactive overview of company sales activity using transaction-level data. The dashboard highlights key performance indicators including total sales, order volume, product category performance, and customer contribution. The analysis reveals that total sales reached $10.03 million across 307 orders, with approximately 99,000 units sold. Product category analysis shows that Classic Cars dominate the revenue contribution, while geographical analysis indicates that the United States generates the highest sales among all countries. The dashboard enables decision-makers to monitor sales trends, identify top-performing products and customers, and evaluate regional sales distribution to support data-driven business strategies.
