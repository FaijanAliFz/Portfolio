This project involves analyzing employee performance, attendance, and sales data to derive meaningful insights for organizational decision-making. The analysis focuses on identifying key contributors, understanding attendance trends, and evaluating managerial and departmental performance. Key areas of focus include determining top-performing employees and managers, analyzing attendance rates, understanding bonus allocation patterns, and assessing department-wide sales and performance trends. Additionally, the project identifies employees with high absenteeism, those who exceed departmental performance averages, and employees with low performance based on sales and attendance metrics. By leveraging these insights, the project aims to enhance employee management strategies, improve team productivity, and support data-driven decision-making to foster organizational growth.
