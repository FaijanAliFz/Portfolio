This project involves a comprehensive analysis of customer orders and product sales within an e-commerce database. It leverages SQL queries to extract valuable insights that can guide business decision-making in areas such as customer retention, product performance, and revenue generation. The queries focus on understanding various aspects of business performance, including customer spending, order frequency, product sales, and regional trends. Insights gained from the data analysis can inform marketing strategies, inventory management, and overall business operations. The results provide a detailed view of which customers are driving the most revenue, which products are performing the best, and how orders are distributed across different time periods and locations. By analyzing customer behaviors, product sales, and transaction patterns, this project helps to uncover trends that can be used to optimize sales strategies, improve customer engagement, and streamline operations. The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights that support business growth and enhance customer satisfaction.
